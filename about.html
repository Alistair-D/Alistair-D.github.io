About My Life:

I was born in Janesville, Wisconsin. Although, my family is originally South African! I am the first American in my bloodline, supposedly! I attended St. Mary’s Elementary, St. Paul's, and Craig High school (in that specific order). I ‘ve traveled on trips all across America and Southern Africa. As of writing this, I am 15 years old. However, that’ll change in January.

My fascination with Science stemmed from my very first years. I was first introduced to Science through Museums. Natural History fascinates me the most, and it drives my curiosity. Then, at St Mary’s, they began STEM robotics in my elementary years. From what I remember, this was my first introduction to computer science. I then moved into actual computer science classes at St. Paul’s. I also started a career in fossil collecting/research. Both of which have made me a lot more knowledgeable. Now as a 9th grader (2025), I have done both Computer Programming and Web Design. I’ve proved my abilities in both!
